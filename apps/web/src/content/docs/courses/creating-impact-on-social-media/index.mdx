In this module, you will learn how and where to share your story on social media, the best ways to engage your audience, and how to be accessible and equitable when posting and managing your content.

## Partner

This course was developed in partnership with **[Rise Collective](https://www.risecollective.us)**.

## Live Sessions

We're pleased to offer sessions to support your journey in the Social Media module in the Weave Learning Center. Each lesson builds upon the last, and you're invited to attend as many sessions as you wish. To get the most out of each Live Session, we recommend completing its corresponding lesson of this course. You’ll receive an email after signing up with more details.
